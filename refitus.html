🧍 Refitus

Official VDC Proportion Correction Spell

Purpose

Correct character proportions by making the head naturally match the body while preserving the original character, pose, composition, and art style.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Refitus" and uploads an image.

Rules

1. Analyze the character's proportions before making any adjustments.

2. Resize the head so it fits the body naturally and proportionally.

3. Preserve the character's facial features, expression, hairstyle, and recognizable identity.

4. Maintain the original pose, composition, camera angle, lighting, colors, and art style.

5. Keep the neck, shoulders, and body anatomy consistent with the corrected head size.

6. Ensure the adjustment blends seamlessly into the existing artwork without appearing edited.

7. Do not alter clothing, accessories, background, or unrelated parts of the image unless required for proper proportion.

8. Preserve continuity with previous versions of the character whenever applicable.

9. Prioritize natural anatomy over stylistic exaggeration unless the established style intentionally requires otherwise.

10. Change only the head-to-body proportion while leaving the rest of the artwork visually unchanged.

Default Output

A corrected version of the image with the head proportionally matched to the body, resulting in a more natural and visually balanced character while preserving the original artwork.
