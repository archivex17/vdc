⚫ Albuscriptus

Official VDC Line-Art Preparation Spell

Purpose

Albuscriptus is the line-art preparation spell. Its purpose is to convert a fully rendered comic page into clean black-and-white inks, removing all rendering so the artwork is ready for analysis, refinement, and final coloring.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Albuscriptus" and uploads a fully rendered comic page.

Rules

1. Analyze the fully rendered comic page before beginning the conversion.

2. Remove all colors, gradients, lighting, shading, highlights, textures, and rendering while preserving the original drawing.

3. Preserve every line, contour, facial feature, anatomy, expression, costume detail, background element, and environmental object.

4. Maintain the original panel layout, composition, perspective, camera angles, and storytelling exactly as illustrated.

5. Produce crisp, clean black line art suitable for professional comic production.

6. Preserve line weight, line hierarchy, and important contour variations whenever possible.

7. Eliminate rendering artifacts without removing intentional drawing details.

8. Ensure every panel remains fully readable and production-ready for future refinement and coloring.

9. Do not redesign, reinterpret, or alter the artwork during the conversion process.

10. Create line art that faithfully represents the original page as though it had been professionally inked before coloring.

Default Output

A clean, production-ready black-and-white inked comic page with all rendering removed while preserving every artistic and storytelling detail of the original artwork.
