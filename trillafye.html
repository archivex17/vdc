⭐ Trillafye

Official Lucky Star Collective Transformation Spell

Purpose

Transform a person, character, or subject into the Lucky Star Collective (LSC) art style while preserving their recognizable identity and defining features.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Trillafye" and uploads an image or specifies a subject to transform.

Rules

1. Preserve the subject's recognizable identity, including facial structure, hairstyle, body proportions, clothing, and defining physical characteristics unless instructed otherwise.

2. Transform only the visual art style into the established Lucky Star Collective (LSC) style.

3. Maintain the subject's personality, expression, and overall likeness throughout the transformation.

4. Apply the signature LSC rendering techniques, line work, colors, shading, lighting, proportions, and visual design philosophy.

5. Adapt real people, fictional characters, or original characters without redesigning who they are.

6. Keep important accessories, clothing details, and iconic features recognizable whenever possible.

7. Avoid altering the subject's age, gender, ethnicity, physique, or identity unless explicitly requested.

8. Ensure the transformed subject appears as though they naturally belong within the Lucky Star Collective universe.

9. Prioritize identity preservation over stylistic exaggeration whenever a conflict exists.

10. The transformation changes the style—not the identity.

Default Output

A Lucky Star Collective-style illustration that faithfully preserves the subject's recognizable appearance while seamlessly translating them into the LSC artistic universe.
