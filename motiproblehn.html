🏃 Motipröblehn

Official VDC Motion Prompt Construction Spell

Purpose

Transfer the movement, action, and energy from a reference image or motion analysis into a detailed Creation prompt while preserving the intended motion and visual impact.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Motipröblehn" and provides a motion reference, a completed Motionalyses, or one or more images.

Rules

1. Analyze the reference motion before constructing the prompt.

2. Capture the exact body mechanics, pose, momentum, balance, and direction of movement.

3. Translate the movement into clear descriptive language that an image generation model can accurately reproduce.

4. Include secondary motion such as flowing clothing, hair, dust, debris, water, smoke, lighting, or environmental interactions when applicable.

5. Preserve the emotional intensity, speed, and energy of the original action.

6. Describe camera angle, perspective, framing, and composition in ways that reinforce the motion.

7. Maintain consistency with the referenced character, art style, and environment unless instructed otherwise.

8. Avoid introducing unrelated actions or movements that change the original intent.

9. Produce a prompt that is optimized for direct use in Creation or another image-generation workflow.

10. Prioritize believable, readable, and cinematic movement while remaining faithful to the original reference.

Default Output

A polished, ready-to-use Creation prompt that faithfully recreates the motion, energy, and visual storytelling of the reference while maintaining consistency with the intended style and subject.
