🎨 Chromitus

Official VDC Color Correction Spell

Purpose

Correct and unify color consistency throughout an image while preserving the original rendering, anatomy, composition, lighting, and artistic style.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Chromitus" and uploads an image.

Rules

1. Analyze the entire image before making any color adjustments.

2. Correct inconsistent colors while preserving the original rendering style and artistic intent.

3. Maintain the existing anatomy, proportions, composition, perspective, and camera angle.

4. Preserve lighting, shadows, highlights, textures, and material rendering unless color corrections require minor adjustments.

5. Ensure recurring characters, clothing, objects, and environments retain their established color schemes.

6. Eliminate unintended color shifts, mismatched hues, oversaturation, undersaturation, and inconsistent tones.

7. Match colors to previously established references whenever applicable.

8. Avoid redesigning or recoloring intentional artistic choices unless specifically requested.

9. Ensure every color correction blends naturally with the surrounding artwork.

10. Change only color consistency while leaving every other aspect of the image visually unchanged.

Default Output

A color-corrected version of the original image with consistent, accurate colors while preserving the original rendering, anatomy, composition, and artistic identity.
