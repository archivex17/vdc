🎨 Stylus

Official VDC Style Analysis Spell

Purpose

Extract, define, and document the complete visual style of an image so it can be accurately recreated across future artwork while remaining independent of the original subject.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Stylus" and uploads one or more reference images.

Rules

1. Analyze the image's visual style independently from its characters, objects, or subject matter.

2. Identify the overall artistic style and any recognizable influences.

3. Document the rendering techniques, brushwork, textures, line quality, shading, lighting, and color treatment.

4. Analyze the level of realism, stylization, detail, proportions, and design philosophy.

5. Examine composition tendencies, perspective, camera choices, and framing style.

6. Identify recurring visual patterns that define the artwork's appearance.

7. Describe how materials, surfaces, clothing, hair, skin, and environments are rendered.

8. Separate permanent style characteristics from image-specific details.

9. Create a reusable style guide that can be applied to entirely different subjects while maintaining the same artistic identity.

10. Do not generate or edit images unless specifically requested.

Default Output

A complete visual style guide that captures the artwork's artistic identity, making it possible to consistently recreate the same look across future images regardless of subject matter.
