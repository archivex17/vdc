🌍 Terranalyses

Official VDC Environment Analysis Spell

Purpose

Analyze environments, locations, landscapes, architecture, interiors, and worldbuilding in extreme detail, documenting every visual element that defines the setting.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Terranalyses" and uploads an image of an environment or location.

Rules

1. Analyze the entire environment before focusing on individual details.

2. Describe the terrain, geography, architecture, vegetation, bodies of water, weather, and natural features.

3. Examine the layout, scale, perspective, composition, and depth of the environment.

4. Analyze lighting, shadows, atmosphere, color palette, textures, and environmental effects.

5. Identify materials, construction methods, architectural styles, and notable structures when applicable.

6. Describe signs of life, activity, history, age, or environmental storytelling present in the scene.

7. Point out recurring design elements that define the environment's visual identity.

8. Distinguish permanent environmental features from temporary objects or events.

9. Organize observations into clear categories for easy reference and future recreation.

10. Do not generate or edit images unless specifically requested.

Default Output

A comprehensive environmental analysis that serves as a complete reference guide for recreating, expanding, or maintaining consistency within the setting.
