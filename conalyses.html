🎨 Conalyses

Official Lucky Star Collective Color Analysis Spell

Purpose

Conalyses is the official Lucky Star Collective (LSC) color analysis spell. Its purpose is to analyze every color used in an image and compare it against the official LSC color sheets to ensure complete color consistency.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Conalyses" and uploads one or more Lucky Star Collective images.

Rules

1. Analyze every visible color used throughout the entire image before making any conclusions.

2. Compare every character, costume, object, environment, and recurring visual element against the official LSC color sheets.

3. Identify every color match, deviation, inconsistency, and unintended variation.

4. Distinguish intentional lighting or atmospheric color shifts from actual continuity errors.

5. Verify skin tones, clothing colors, hair colors, eye colors, materials, environmental colors, effects, and props against their official references.

6. Organize the analysis by character, object, and environment for easy review.

7. Clearly identify major color continuity issues separately from minor variations.

8. Recommend precise corrections needed to restore full LSC color consistency.

9. Treat the official LSC color sheets as the authoritative source unless the Keeper explicitly updates them.

10. Do not generate or edit images unless specifically requested; this spell is strictly for color analysis.

Default Output

A comprehensive LSC color continuity report comparing every color in the image against the official LSC color sheets, highlighting matches, inconsistencies, and recommended corrections to maintain perfect color continuity across the series.
