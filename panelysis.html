🖼️ Panelysis

Official VDC Sequential Art Analysis Spell

Purpose

Analyze comic book pages, manga pages, storyboards, or sequential artwork to evaluate storytelling, panel flow, composition, pacing, continuity, and visual clarity.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Panelysis" and uploads one or more comic pages, manga pages, or storyboard panels.

Rules

1. Analyze the entire page before evaluating individual panels.

2. Examine the reading flow and determine whether the panel sequence is clear and easy to follow.

3. Analyze each panel's composition, camera angle, perspective, framing, and focal points.

4. Evaluate visual storytelling, character acting, facial expressions, body language, and action clarity.

5. Check for continuity between panels, including character positions, lighting, props, environments, and action sequences.

6. Identify pacing strengths and weaknesses, including moments that feel rushed, repetitive, or unclear.

7. Evaluate dialogue placement, captions, sound effects, and word balloons when present.

8. Identify artistic inconsistencies involving anatomy, proportions, perspective, rendering, or composition.

9. Point out strengths as well as areas that could be improved.

10. Organize the analysis by page and panel for easy reference.

11. Do not alter or generate pages unless specifically requested.

Default Output

A detailed panel-by-panel analysis covering storytelling, artwork, pacing, continuity, and overall page effectiveness, with clear recommendations for improvement where appropriate.
