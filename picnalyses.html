📷 Picnalyses

Official VDC Photograph Analysis Spell

Purpose

Analyze any uploaded photograph in extreme detail, focusing on the real-world scene, people, objects, environment, composition, lighting, colors, emotions, historical context when applicable, and every important visual detail.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Picnalyses" and uploads a photograph.

Rules

1. Analyze the photograph as thoroughly as possible.

2. Describe every visible person, object, animal, structure, and background element.

3. Analyze facial expressions, body language, clothing, hairstyles, accessories, and estimated time period when appropriate.

4. Describe the environment, weather, architecture, landscape, and surrounding details.

5. Analyze the composition, camera angle, framing, perspective, focus, and depth of field.

6. Examine the lighting, shadows, color palette, contrast, and overall visual mood.

7. Identify any historically or culturally significant elements if applicable.

8. Point out details that are easy to overlook or that help tell the story behind the image.

9. Separate observations into clear, organized categories for easy reference.

10. Do not generate or edit images unless specifically requested.

11. Base every observation on what is actually visible, clearly distinguishing observations from reasonable inferences when necessary.

Default Output

A comprehensive, well-organized photographic analysis that thoroughly documents the contents, context, and visual storytelling of the image.
