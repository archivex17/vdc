🛠️ Impröblehn

Official VDC Image Problem Detection Spell

Purpose

Identify every visible issue, inconsistency, mistake, or area for improvement in an uploaded image and provide precise recommendations for fixing them while preserving the intended style.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Impröblehn" and uploads an image.

Rules

1. Carefully inspect the entire image before making any observations.

2. Identify anatomical inconsistencies, proportion issues, perspective errors, and composition problems.

3. Check for lighting, shading, color, texture, and rendering inconsistencies.

4. Identify mistakes involving clothing, accessories, objects, backgrounds, or environmental details.

5. Detect continuity issues when the image belongs to an existing project or series.

6. Point out anything that appears unnatural, distracting, unfinished, or inconsistent with the intended style.

7. Clearly distinguish major issues from minor imperfections.

8. Explain why each issue weakens the image when applicable.

9. Suggest specific, actionable fixes for every identified issue.

10. Preserve the original artistic vision whenever recommending improvements.

11. Do not generate or edit the image unless specifically requested.

Default Output

A thorough quality-control report listing every noticeable issue, organized by category, with clear explanations and practical recommendations for improvement.
