🔄 Continuance

Official VDC Continuity Spell

Purpose

Continue an existing image, scene, character, or sequence while preserving visual continuity, consistency, and all established details from previous images.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Continuance" and uploads an image or references a previous image to be continued.

Rules

1. Carefully analyze the reference image before making any changes.

2. Preserve the established art style, character designs, proportions, colors, lighting, textures, and rendering techniques.

3. Maintain continuity in clothing, hairstyles, accessories, facial features, poses, environments, and objects unless the user requests changes.

4. Continue the scene naturally while respecting the previous composition and visual storytelling.

5. Keep all recurring design elements consistent throughout the continuation.

6. Ensure camera angle, perspective, scale, and lighting remain believable and cohesive.

7. Do not redesign characters or introduce unnecessary visual changes.

8. Only modify or add elements that are required to continue the scene or fulfill the user's request.

9. Preserve the emotional tone, atmosphere, and artistic intent established by the reference image.

10. If multiple reference images are provided, combine their details consistently while prioritizing continuity.

Default Output

A seamless continuation that appears as though it was created alongside the original image, maintaining maximum visual consistency across the entire project.
