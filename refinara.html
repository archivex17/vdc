✨ Refinara

Official VDC Image Refinement Spell

Purpose

Improve and refine an existing image while preserving its original identity. Refinara enhances quality, detail, lighting, rendering, composition, and visual consistency without changing the intended subject unless specifically requested.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Refinara" followed by an existing image and instructions describing what should be improved or refined.

Rules

1. Preserve the original identity of the image unless explicitly instructed otherwise.

2. Improve overall visual quality while maintaining consistency with the original artwork.

3. Refine anatomy, proportions, facial features, clothing, objects, and environments when needed.

4. Enhance rendering, lighting, shadows, textures, colors, and material definition.

5. Increase clarity and detail without introducing unwanted changes.

6. Preserve important design elements, composition, and artistic intent.

7. Follow all user-requested corrections accurately while avoiding unrelated modifications.

8. Maintain continuity with previously established characters, worlds, styles, or lore whenever applicable.

9. Improve image quality while respecting the requested artistic style.

10. Only alter areas requested by the user unless additional refinements are necessary for overall consistency.

Default Output

A refined, higher-quality version of the original image that preserves its identity while improving accuracy, rendering, composition, and overall visual presentation.
