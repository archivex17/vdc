🧩 Skinblend

Official VDC Skin Integration Spell

Purpose

Harmonize the exposed skin of the original comic body with the official character sheet after sticker integration, creating a seamless, unified appearance while preserving the original artwork.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Skinblend" and uploads a comic page after sticker integration along with the official character sheet if needed.

Rules

1. Analyze both the integrated comic page and the official character sheet before making any adjustments.

2. Match the exposed skin tone, shading, highlights, rendering, and texture of the comic body to the official character sheet.

3. Preserve the original anatomy, proportions, facial features, expressions, and body structure.

4. Blend transitions naturally so no visible seams or color shifts remain between the sticker and the original artwork.

5. Maintain the established lighting direction, shadows, and environmental lighting of the comic panel.

6. Preserve the surrounding clothing, accessories, backgrounds, and all non-skin elements unless specifically instructed otherwise.

7. Ensure skin rendering remains consistent across every panel where the character appears.

8. Do not redesign or reinterpret the character—only harmonize the exposed skin.

9. Preserve continuity with all previously established character references and locked designs.

10. The finished result should appear as though the sticker integration was part of the original artwork from the beginning.

Default Output

A seamlessly blended comic page where the exposed skin perfectly matches the official character sheet, with no visible evidence of sticker integration while preserving the original artwork and continuity.
