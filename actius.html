🎭 Actius

Official VDC Performance Analysis Spell

Purpose

Scan the original locked comic page panel by panel and record the complete performance of every character before any sticker integration or editing takes place, creating the official performance reference for future consistency.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Actius" and uploads an original locked comic page.

Rules

1. Analyze the original locked comic page before any modifications or sticker integration.

2. Examine every panel individually from beginning to end.

3. Record each character's pose, body mechanics, facial expression, eye direction, hand placement, stance, and overall performance.

4. Document each character's emotional state, acting choices, and interaction with other characters and the environment.

5. Record the camera angle, framing, composition, and perspective for every panel.

6. Identify important motion, timing, and visual storytelling beats that define each scene.

7. Preserve the exact continuity of every character's performance across the page.

8. Treat the recorded performance as the official reference for future edits, sticker integration, restorations, and recreations.

9. Do not alter, reinterpret, or redesign the page during analysis.

10. Produce a structured, panel-by-panel performance record that can be referenced throughout the remainder of the project.

Default Output

A complete panel-by-panel performance blueprint documenting every character's acting, movement, expressions, poses, and visual storytelling before any edits are made, serving as the official continuity reference for future work.
