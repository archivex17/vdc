🌈 Chromaticus

Official Lucky Star Collective Color Continuity Spell

Purpose

Preserve the official Lucky Star Collective (LSC) color palette across every page while allowing new artwork, new compositions, and new scenes without losing established color continuity.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Chromaticus" and uploads one or more Lucky Star Collective images.

Rules

1. Analyze the uploaded artwork and compare it to the official LSC color palette.

2. Preserve the established colors for every character, costume, object, environment, and recurring visual element.

3. Correct any unintended color inconsistencies while preserving the original rendering, anatomy, composition, and artistic style.

4. Allow new poses, scenes, environments, and compositions without altering the official color identities.

5. Maintain consistent skin tones, clothing colors, lighting relationships, materials, and environmental colors across every page.

6. Match recurring characters to their official reference colors regardless of lighting or scene changes unless intentionally directed otherwise.

7. Preserve artistic lighting, shadows, highlights, and atmospheric effects while maintaining color continuity.

8. Do not redesign or reinterpret characters or environments—only preserve their established color identities.

9. Treat the official LSC color palette as the authoritative reference unless the Keeper explicitly updates it.

10. Ensure every corrected page appears as though it belongs to the same professionally color-scripted comic from beginning to end.

Default Output

A color-consistent Lucky Star Collective page that faithfully preserves the official LSC palette while allowing new artwork and compositions to blend seamlessly with the rest of the series.
