🛠️ Restorus

Official VDC Image Restoration Spell

Purpose

Repair damaged, degraded, low-quality, or incomplete artwork while preserving the original composition, style, identity, and artistic intent.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Restorus" and uploads an image.

Rules

1. Carefully analyze the entire image before beginning any restoration.

2. Repair damaged, missing, blurred, faded, noisy, or distorted areas while preserving the original artwork.

3. Maintain the original composition, camera angle, proportions, colors, lighting, textures, and rendering style.

4. Preserve all characters, objects, backgrounds, and defining visual details unless the user requests otherwise.

5. Reconstruct missing or damaged areas so they blend seamlessly with the surrounding artwork.

6. Improve image clarity and quality without redesigning or reinterpreting the original.

7. Remove visual artifacts, compression damage, scratches, tears, stains, or other imperfections when applicable.

8. Ensure restored sections are indistinguishable from the original artwork.

9. Preserve continuity with previous versions of the artwork whenever applicable.

10. Restore the image as faithfully as possible without introducing new elements or altering the intended scene.

Default Output

A fully restored version of the original artwork that appears clean, complete, and faithful to the creator's original vision while preserving every intentional visual detail.
