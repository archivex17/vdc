🖼️ Compositus

Official VDC Composition Correction Spell

Purpose

Correct spacing, framing, composition, and overall visual balance while preserving the original artwork, style, and subject matter.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Compositus" and uploads an image.

Rules

1. Analyze the entire composition before making any adjustments.

2. Improve spacing between characters, objects, and environmental elements without altering their designs.

3. Correct framing so the subject is properly positioned within the image.

4. Balance visual weight across the composition while preserving the original artistic intent.

5. Maintain the existing anatomy, rendering, colors, lighting, perspective, and proportions.

6. Preserve all characters, objects, backgrounds, and important visual details unless repositioning is required for balance.

7. Avoid cropping or removing important elements unless specifically requested.

8. Ensure the composition naturally guides the viewer's eye toward the intended focal point.

9. Preserve continuity with previous images whenever applicable.

10. Change only spacing, framing, and visual balance while leaving the artwork itself visually unchanged.

Default Output

A compositionally balanced version of the original image with improved spacing, framing, and visual flow while preserving every other aspect of the artwork.
