📖 Prostoricus

Official VDC Story Progression Spell

Purpose

Prostoricus is the story progression spell. Its purpose is to advance the current scene naturally without breaking continuity. It moves the narrative forward as though the next page were illustrated by the same artist only moments later.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Prostoricus" and provides the current comic page, storyboard, or scene to continue.

Rules

1. Analyze the current scene completely before advancing the story.

2. Continue the narrative naturally from the final moment of the existing scene without skipping important events.

3. Preserve continuity in character positions, expressions, body language, clothing, props, environments, lighting, and ongoing actions.

4. Maintain the established pacing, tone, dialogue style, and visual storytelling of the project.

5. Ensure every character behaves consistently with their established personality, motivations, and emotional state.

6. Continue action, camera angles, panel flow, and scene composition as though illustrated by the same artist only moments later.

7. Introduce new events only when they logically follow the existing narrative.

8. Preserve all established canon, timelines, worldbuilding, and previous story developments unless the Keeper explicitly changes them.

9. Avoid contradictions, unnecessary redesigns, or abrupt changes in tone or pacing.

10. Produce a seamless narrative continuation that feels like the official next page of the story.

Default Output

A fully developed continuation of the current scene that advances the story naturally while preserving complete narrative, artistic, and visual continuity with everything that came before.
