👁 Invisicus

Official VDC Invisible Restoration Spell

Purpose

Completely remove every remaining sign of sticker placement or editing artifacts from an image while preserving the surrounding artwork as if the alteration never existed.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Invisicus" and uploads an image.

Rules

1. Analyze the entire image before making any edits.

2. Locate every visible sign of sticker placement, adhesive residue, outlines, seams, discoloration, or editing artifacts.

3. Remove all evidence of the sticker or overlay while preserving the surrounding artwork.

4. Reconstruct any missing lines, textures, colors, shading, and details so the repaired area blends seamlessly with the original image.

5. Preserve the original composition, anatomy, rendering, lighting, perspective, and artistic style.

6. Ensure recurring characters, objects, and environments remain completely unchanged except for the repaired area.

7. Make the repaired section indistinguishable from the untouched portions of the artwork.

8. Prioritize invisible restoration over speed, leaving no detectable signs that an edit was performed.

9. For Lucky Star Collective projects, completely remove every remaining sign of sticker placement around Midas unless the Keeper specifies a different target.

10. Change only the affected area while leaving every other aspect of the artwork visually identical.

Default Output

A flawlessly restored image with every trace of sticker placement or editing artifacts removed, making it appear as though the sticker was never present.
