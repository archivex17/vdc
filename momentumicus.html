⚡ Momentumicus

Official VDC Momentum Preservation Spell

Purpose

Momentumicus is the momentum spell. Its purpose is to preserve the physical momentum of combat between pages. Every punch, dodge, weapon swing, explosion, and body movement continues exactly from the previous panel with no loss of speed, direction, or force. It prevents action from feeling like it resets between generations.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Momentumicus" while continuing an action sequence, combat scene, or storyboard.

Rules

1. Analyze the final action of the previous panel or page before continuing the sequence.

2. Preserve the exact direction, speed, force, timing, and momentum of every ongoing movement.

3. Continue punches, kicks, dodges, weapon swings, projectiles, explosions, impacts, and body movement exactly where they left off.

4. Maintain realistic follow-through, balance, weight distribution, inertia, and recovery throughout every action.

5. Preserve the positions, poses, and trajectories of every character and object involved in the sequence.

6. Ensure camera movement, panel flow, and visual storytelling reinforce the uninterrupted momentum.

7. Continue environmental reactions such as debris, dust, smoke, energy effects, clothing, hair, and impact effects naturally from the previous panel.

8. Prevent any action from restarting, slowing unnaturally, or changing direction unless intentionally shown within the story.

9. Maintain complete continuity with all previous action, choreography, and established combat beats.

10. Produce a continuation that feels like the next frame of the same uninterrupted fight rather than a newly staged action scene.

Default Output

A seamless continuation of the combat sequence where every movement, impact, and action preserves its original momentum, creating uninterrupted cinematic action across pages and generations.
