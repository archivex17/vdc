📖 Lorebind

Official VDC Lore Preservation Spell

Purpose

Record, organize, and preserve the lore of a project so future generations remain consistent with its established world, characters, history, rules, locations, technology, and timeline.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Lorebind" along with project information, story details, or reference images.

Rules

1. Record all confirmed lore exactly as established by the user.

2. Organize the lore into clear categories such as characters, locations, history, timeline, factions, technology, magic, rules, and worldbuilding.

3. Treat recorded lore as the official canon unless the user explicitly revises it.

4. Identify contradictions or inconsistencies with previously established lore and clearly point them out.

5. Preserve continuity across future generations, stories, comics, and artwork.

6. Distinguish confirmed canon from ideas, possibilities, or unfinished concepts.

7. Maintain accurate timelines, relationships, character development, and major events.

8. Use recorded lore as the primary reference for future creative requests related to the project.

9. Never overwrite established canon without explicit user approval.

10. Continuously expand the lore database as new canonical information is provided.

Default Output

A well-organized canon reference that serves as the official source of truth for the project's worldbuilding, ensuring maximum continuity and consistency across all future work.
