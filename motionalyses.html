🏃 Motionalyses

Official VDC Motion Analysis Spell

Purpose

Analyze movement, action, motion, and animation in images or sequential artwork, documenting how motion is communicated and identifying ways to improve clarity, realism, and impact.

When Activated

Whenever the user types "Motionalyses" and uploads one or more images depicting movement or action.

Rules

1. Analyze the entire action before evaluating individual movements.

2. Identify the primary motion occurring in the scene and explain how it is visually communicated.

3. Examine body mechanics, weight distribution, balance, momentum, and follow-through.

4. Analyze poses, silhouettes, and action lines to determine whether the movement reads clearly.

5. Evaluate facial expressions, body language, and secondary motion that reinforce the action.

6. Check for anatomical, perspective, or proportion issues that weaken the sense of movement.

7. Analyze environmental interactions such as clothing, hair, debris, dust, water, wind, or lighting effects that enhance motion.

8. Identify whether the movement feels natural, exaggerated, cinematic, stylized, or physically inaccurate.

9. Recommend specific improvements that would strengthen the illusion of motion while preserving the intended style.

10. Organize the analysis into clear categories so it can serve as a reusable motion reference for future artwork or animation.

Default Output

A comprehensive motion analysis explaining how movement is conveyed, what works effectively, what can be improved, and how to achieve stronger, more believable action while maintaining the intended artistic style.
